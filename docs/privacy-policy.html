Prokolpo Bondhu Privacy Policy

Introduction

Prokolpo Bondhu is an independent information application that provides information about government schemes, eligibility criteria, required documents, official resources, and related educational video guides.

Prokolpo Bondhu is not an official government application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or operated by any government department, ministry, agency, or public authority.

Information We Collect

Prokolpo Bondhu does not collect personally identifiable information directly through the application, including:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Aadhaar Number
  • Bank Account Details
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address (unless you voluntarily contact us)
  • Passwords or OTPs

Voluntary Communication

If you choose to contact us through WhatsApp or email, you may voluntarily provide your name, phone number, email address, message, or other information necessary to respond to your inquiry.

Such communication is handled by the respective third-party service providers according to their own privacy policies.

Please do not send sensitive personal information such as Aadhaar numbers, bank account details, passwords, PINs, or OTPs through WhatsApp or email.

Local Storage

To improve performance and provide a better user experience, the application may store scheme-related information locally on your device. This locally stored data is used only for application functionality and is not used to identify you.

Internet Usage

The application requires an internet connection to retrieve updated scheme information, access official government resources, display video-related content, and check for application updates. Internet access is used solely to provide these features and improve the overall user experience.

Third-Party Services

Prokolpo Bondhu may use or provide access to third-party services to support certain features of the application, including:

  • GitHub Pages for hosting privacy policies, terms, and other public information pages
  • YouTube for educational videos and video-related content
  • WhatsApp for optional customer support and communication
  • Official government websites and portals for scheme information, resources, and application services

These third-party services operate independently and may collect or process information according to their own privacy policies. Prokolpo Bondhu does not control and is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or availability of any third-party websites, applications, or services. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any third-party service they choose to access.

Government Information Disclaimer

Information about government schemes provided through Prokolpo Bondhu is collected from publicly available sources and official government websites.

Scheme details, eligibility criteria, benefits, required documents, application procedures, deadlines, and other information may change at any time without prior notice.

Users should always verify the latest information directly from the relevant official government website before making any decisions or submitting an application.

Prokolpo Bondhu does not represent any government authority and does not guarantee eligibility, approval, payments, benefits, or successful application outcomes.

Children's Privacy

Prokolpo Bondhu does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the application after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
