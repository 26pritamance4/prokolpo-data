Prokolpo Bondhu Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

Prokolpo Bondhu is an independent information application that provides information about government schemes, eligibility criteria, required documents, official resources, and related video guides.

Prokolpo Bondhu is not an official government application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any government department, agency, or public authority.

Information We Collect

Prokolpo Bondhu does not collect personally identifiable information directly through the app, such as:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Aadhaar number
  • Bank details
  • Phone number
  • Passwords or OTPs

Voluntary Communication

If users choose to contact us through WhatsApp or email, they may voluntarily share their phone number, name, message, or other information. Such communication is handled by WhatsApp, email providers, and other third-party services according to their own privacy policies.

Users should not share Aadhaar numbers, bank details, passwords, OTPs, or other sensitive personal information through WhatsApp or email.

Local Storage

The application may store scheme information locally on your device to improve performance and provide better access to previously loaded information. This locally stored scheme data is not used to identify you.

Internet Usage

The application uses internet access to fetch updated scheme information, open official resources, load video thumbnails or video links, and check application updates.

Third-Party Services

The application may use or link to third-party services, including:

  • GitHub and GitHub Pages for scheme data, app update information, and policy pages
  • YouTube or YouTube-related services for video guides and video thumbnails
  • WhatsApp for optional user support
  • Expo and EAS Updates for application update delivery
  • Official government websites or portals for scheme resources and applications

These third-party services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. Prokolpo Bondhu is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites, apps, or services.

Government Information Disclaimer

Information about government schemes is collected from publicly available sources. Scheme details, eligibility rules, benefits, documents, deadlines, and application processes may change at any time.

Users should verify all details from official government portals before applying. Prokolpo Bondhu does not represent any government authority and does not guarantee scheme approval, benefits, payments, or eligibility decisions.

Children's Privacy

Prokolpo Bondhu does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
