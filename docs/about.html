About Prokolpo Bondhu

Prokolpo Bondhu is an independent information application created to help people easily discover information about government schemes.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make government scheme information simple, accessible, and understandable for everyone.

What You Can Find

Important Disclaimer

Prokolpo Bondhu is not an official government application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by any government organization, department, agency, or public authority.

Information is collected from publicly available sources. Users should confirm final details from official government portals before applying.

Prokolpo Bondhu does not guarantee scheme approval, benefits, payments, eligibility decisions, or application processing timelines.
