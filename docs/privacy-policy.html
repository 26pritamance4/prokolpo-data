Prokolpo Bondhu is an independent information application that provides information about government schemes, eligibility criteria, required documents and related resources.

Prokolpo Bondhu is not an official government application and is not affiliated with any government department.

Information We Collect

Prokolpo Bondhu does not collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Address
  • Aadhaar number
  • Bank details
  • Phone number

Local Storage

The application may store scheme information locally on your device to improve performance and provide better access.

Internet Usage

The application uses internet access to fetch updated scheme information, provide official resources and check application updates.

Third Party Services

The application may contain links to third party websites including government websites and YouTube videos. These services have their own privacy policies.

Government Information Disclaimer

Information about government schemes is collected from publicly available sources. Users should verify details from official government portals before applying.

Children's Privacy

Prokolpo Bondhu does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
